🌺 Pig Out at the Yard

Tentative: Saturday, August 22

A Hawaiian-inspired community pig roast at the Yard with island music, tropical fun, and a whole pig roasting outside on a rotisserie where guests can see the action.

We’re planning limbo contests, tiki torches, calypso or Hawaiian-style music, and a relaxed Saturday gathering for families, neighbors, and anyone ready to pig out at the Yard.

Hawaiian shirts, grass skirts, floral prints, leis, flip-flops, and vacation-mode outfits are encouraged.

Pig roast Limbo contest Island vibes

🏕️ The Slightly Haunted Yard Campout 👻

Tentative: September 26–27, 2026

Pack your tents, grab your sleeping bags, and bring your favorite family-friendly costumes for an overnight campout at the Yard.

Planned activities may include costume contests by age group, trick-or-treating around the campsites, a slightly haunted trail, campfire and s’mores under the stars, dinner, breakfast, and overnight camping at the Yard.

Campers are encouraged to decorate their tents and bring candy for trick-or-treaters who will visit the campsites, check out the decorated tents, and vote for their favorites.

More details coming soon as the schedule, pricing, food, and overnight details are finalized.

Family campout Sept. 26–27 Decorated tents Bring candy Spooky-ish fun
