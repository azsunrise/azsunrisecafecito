🌺 Pig Out at the Yard

Date TBD • Moving to a cooler date

Pig Out at the Yard is still on the ideas board, but we’re moving the date so everyone can enjoy it when the weather feels better.

The plan is still a Hawaiian-inspired community pig roast with island music, tropical fun, and a whole pig roasting outside on a rotisserie where guests can see the action.

Hawaiian shirts, grass skirts, floral prints, leis, flip-flops, and vacation-mode outfits will be encouraged once the new date is set.

Date TBD Being rescheduled Pig roast Island vibes

🏈 Sunday Football at the Yard

Begins Sunday, September 13, 2026 • 12:00 PM games only

We’ll be showing the noon Sunday football games at the Yard during the regular season.

Bring the family, grab lunch, hang out, and catch the game in a relaxed outdoor setting.

Game availability may vary by broadcast schedule.

☕🚗 Coffee, Cars & Cafecito

Moved to Saturday, September 19, 2026 • 8:30–11 AM

Coffee, Cars & Cafecito has been moved to September 19 because the summer heat has been too much for a morning cruise-in.

Roll into the Yard for a casual Saturday cruise-in with cars, coffee, breakfast, brunch, and community.

Classic cars, Jeeps, trucks, motorcycles, family rides, and daily drivers are all welcome — no trophies, no pressure, just a fun morning at the Yard.

Moved to Sept. 19 No trophies No pressure Coffee + brunch

🛍🔥 Campfire & Crafts Market

Saturday, October 10, 2026 • 11:00 AM–7:00 PM

Not your typical vendor market — spend the day shopping local, grabbing food from the trucks, relaxing around the campfire, and making memories with family and friends.

Planned activities include local shopping, food trucks, campfire lunch and dinner, s’mores, campfire stories, family-friendly fun, and unique vendors and small businesses.

Campfire schedule: lunch by the campfire from 12:00–1:00 PM, dinner by the campfire from 5:00–6:00 PM, and s’mores and family campfire time from 6:00–7:00 PM.

Free admission and free parking.

Interested in becoming a vendor? Text 903-530-0362 or email TheLocalVendorMarket@gmail.com.

Vendor market Campfire S’mores Free admission Family-friendly

🍂🛍️ Harvest & Homemade Market

Saturday, November 21, 2026 • 9:00 AM–5:00 PM

Celebrate the harvest season and kick off holiday shopping with a full day of local makers, crafters, bakers, boutiques, artists, home décor vendors, food, and family-friendly fun at Holy Grounds Food Yard & Mercantile.

Shop fall and Thanksgiving décor, early Christmas gifts, handmade goods, homemade treats, unique small businesses, and local favorites while enjoying food from the trailers.

Free admission and free parking.

Interested in becoming a vendor? Text 903-530-0362 or email TheLocalVendorMarket@gmail.com.
