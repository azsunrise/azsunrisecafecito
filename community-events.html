Confirmed: Saturday, August 22, 2026

🛍🔥 Campfire & Crafts Market

Saturday, October 10, 2026 • 11:00 AM–7:00 PM

Not your typical vendor market — spend the day shopping local, grabbing food from the trucks, relaxing around the campfire, and making memories with family and friends.

Planned activities include local shopping, food trucks, campfire lunch and dinner, s’mores, campfire stories, family-friendly fun, and unique vendors and small businesses.

Campfire schedule: lunch by the campfire from 12:00–1:00 PM, dinner by the campfire from 5:00–6:00 PM, and s’mores and family campfire time from 6:00–7:00 PM.

Free admission and free parking.

Interested in becoming a vendor? Text 903-530-0362 or email TheLocalVendorMarket@gmail.com.

Vendor market Campfire S’mores Free admission Family-friendly
