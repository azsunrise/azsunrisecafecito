Our Story

Arizona Sunrise Cafecito & Catering is built on experience, family, and the courage to start fresh — serving Royse City with brunch that feels warm, welcoming, and made with care.

Meet Jack & Wendy

Arizona Sunrise began as a shared dream — build something meaningful, serve our community, and make mornings better. Jack (Chef John Ahern) has spent decades in professional kitchens, from resort and country club environments to running his own restaurant and catering operation in Tucson, Arizona.

After closing that chapter, Wendy’s career brought our family to Texas — first to Weston Lakes and later to Heath — where Jack continued as an Executive Chef at Sweetwater Country Club and Pecan Grove.

When we had twins, Jack stepped away from chef roles to become a stay-at-home dad. He even hosted cooking classes at their school — because food has always been about connection for us.

Now that our kids are older and Wendy became retirement-eligible, we decided it was time for a change — and a return to what we’ve always loved: creating food for people, and catering celebrations that bring everyone together.

Jack leads the kitchen. Wendy handles the business side — operations, content, customer communication, and the behind-the-scenes prep and cleaning that keeps everything running smoothly.

What You Can Expect

Simple, satisfying brunch — made fresh and served with heart.

  • Fast, friendly service (perfect for busy mornings)
  • Real kitchen experience behind every item
  • Brunch favorites: burritos, biscuits, pancakes, fresh sides
  • Catering for gatherings & celebrations (we love events)
  • Proudly serving families & hardworking local crews

Why “Arizona Sunrise”?
It honors where our journey began — and represents a fresh start.
