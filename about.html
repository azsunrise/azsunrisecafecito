Our Story

Arizona Sunrise Cafecito & Catering is built on experience, family, and the courage to start fresh — serving Royse City with brunch that feels warm, welcoming, and made with care.

At Arizona Sunrise, we like to say it’s where Arizona spice meets Texas soul. Inspired by years living near the Sonoran border, we blend bold Southwestern flavor with genuine Texas hospitality.

We can turn up the heat or keep it mild — flavor should be bold, never overwhelming. Everything we serve is made fresh in our kitchen — never store-bought. And as our trailer says, the secret ingredient is made with love.

Meet Jack & Wendy

Jack has spent decades in professional kitchens — from Arizona resorts and country clubs to restaurant ownership and catering.

Jack has a gift in the kitchen — everything he touches just tastes better. The first time he cooked Wendy a burger in his backyard, it tasted like steak. That was the moment she thought, “Well… I’m going to marry this man.”

He’ll tell you it’s just good ingredients and technique. We’ll tell you it’s talent.

After raising twins and serving in Executive Chef roles in Texas, we decided it was time to build something together.

Jack leads the kitchen. Wendy leads the business side — operations, content, communication, and the behind-the-scenes work that keeps everything running smoothly.

Catering

We currently specialize in pickup catering for smaller, intimate gatherings — dinner parties, family celebrations, showers, and meaningful events where food truly matters.

As a hands-on, two-person team, this allows us to focus on quality, freshness, and personal attention with every order.

For catering, we focus on pickup large-order trays with advance notice. We’ll share updates as we grow.
