About Arizona Sunrise Cafecito

Arizona Sunrise Cafecito & Catering is a family-owned food trailer bringing bold Arizona flavor together with Texas comfort.

Our menu is inspired by early mornings, hardworking crews, and relaxed weekend brunch — made fresh, served warm, and always with heart.

From cafecito to catered events, our goal is simple: good food, good people, and a welcoming place to start your day.
