Our Story

Arizona Sunrise Cafecito & Catering was created from a love of family, culture, and the joy that comes from sharing good food and coffee.

What started as a dream grew into a place where mornings feel warmer, conversations last longer, and every cup is made with intention.

We believe food should feel like home — welcoming, comforting, and crafted with care. Whether you’re stopping by for cafecito or planning a catered event, we’re honored to be part of your day.

Thank you for supporting a family-owned business and being part of the Arizona Sunrise story.
