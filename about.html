Our Story

Arizona Sunrise Cafecito & Catering is built on experience, family, and the courage to start fresh — serving Royse City with brunch that feels warm, welcoming, and made with care.

At Arizona Sunrise, we like to say it’s where Arizona spice meets Texas soul. Inspired by years living near the Sonoran border, we blend bold Southwestern flavor with genuine Texas hospitality. We can turn up the heat or keep it mild — everything is made fresh in our kitchen. And as our trailer says, the secret ingredient is made with love.

Meet Jack

Jack has spent decades in professional kitchens — from Arizona resorts to restaurant ownership and catering.

Jack has a gift in the kitchen — everything he touches just tastes better. The first time he cooked Wendy a burger in his backyard, it tasted like steak. That was the moment she thought, “Well… I’m going to marry this man.”

He’ll tell you it’s just good ingredients and technique. We’ll tell you it’s talent.

After stepping away to raise their twins, Jack returned to the kitchen with renewed purpose — bringing experience, instinct, and heart into every dish.

Meet Wendy

While Jack leads the kitchen, Wendy leads the business side — overseeing operations, planning, and customer communication. With years of executive leadership experience, she brings strategic organization, disciplined execution, and a servant-leader mindset to Arizona Sunrise.

You’ll also find her working alongside Jack in the kitchen, supporting prep and ensuring each service runs with care and consistency.
